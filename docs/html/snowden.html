Snowden

Who Edward Snowden Is

  • Edward Snowden was a relatively new employee at the National Security Agency (NSA) who leaked one of America's biggest invasive programs.
  • He decided to leak this intel after years of the executive branch being blatantly lying about just how invasive the government was being.

The Program

  • The United States was working on "The Program" since 9/11 to combat terrorism, however it violated the constitutional rights of each United States citizen.
  • The Program wiretapped every call in the US, read everyone's emails, and monitored the online traffic of each citizen.
  • The United States gathered all this information without warrants and used National Security Letters to gather information from companies about their customers' data without them being able to tell anyone due the gag order that came with the letters.
