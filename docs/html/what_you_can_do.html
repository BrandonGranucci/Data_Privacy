Steps To Reduce Your Data Footprint


Since tech companies, hackers, and your governments are constantly trying to spy on you, why don't you take precautions to protect your data?


Moderate Security

  • Use 2 factor authorization for logging into your devices and secure online bank accounts and such
  • Clear your browser caches and cookies occasionally to reduce how much you're tracked
  • Use a Virtual Private Network (VPN) when you browse the web to hide your location and identity
  • Avoid posting your face or any real information about yourself online

Advanced Security

  • The Firefox or Tor browser is much more private than Chrome
  • Proton mail is a more private email service than Gmail
  • Avoid adding untrustworthy addons on your browser
  • Use addons like adblock and https everywhere to reduce the chance of seeing suspicious sites & ads
