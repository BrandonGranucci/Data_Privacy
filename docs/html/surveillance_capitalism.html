Surveillance Capitalism

Misconceptions

  • Companies mask the data they collect using lies, deception, and misdirection.
  • Most data that is collected to be "analyzed" is actually sold for the company's profit.
  • With companies being able to know exactly what devices you use, it makes it much easier for them to know who you are when they cross over that technical data with other information about you like your age, region, and such.

Pokemon Go

  • Pokemon Go was actually started by Google but released under the developer Niantic Labs.
  • Companies can pay to make users travel to or across their business locations.
  • Also Google Earth, which makes Pokemon Go possible, originally called KeyHole was started by the CIA but sold to Google in 2004.


Google Nest

  • The Google Nest Home Security system had a hidden microphone in it which was not disclosed to customers.
  • Google stated that these microphones were added due to an error.

Cambridge Analytica

  • This firm was taking Facebook's user data and was planning on using it for political advertisement.
  • The firm would have made these ads able to alter a person's political views by only using targeted political ads.
  • After a whistleblower disclosed this information, the firm was fined $5 billion and went bankrupt.
