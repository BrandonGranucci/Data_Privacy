Surveillance Capitalism

JavaScript

  • One powerful scripting language to make most websites active, dynamic, and responsive to users is JavaScript.
  • JavaScript however is able to send various data back to websites such as your screen resolution, orientation, any plugins you're using and even your exact device model!
  • With companies being able to know exactly what devices you use, it makes it much easier for them to know who you are when they cross over that technical data with other information about you like your age, region, and such.

Cookies

  • Cookies allow websites to plant unique identifiers onto your device to remember who you are for future logins and such.
  • Although this sounds convenient, websites can use cookies planted by other sites to track where you go on the internet.
  • Unfortunately for users, cookies and JavaScript are an essential part of websites and they would typically cease to work without them.
