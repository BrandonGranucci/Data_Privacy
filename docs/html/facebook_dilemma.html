Facebook Dilemma

Facebook's News Page

  • Initially intended to be a social networking platform, the app took off due to its news page.
  • Since the news page was not like traditional newspapers, users could find any sort of unrestricted content on there posted by anyone.
  • The app first political issue began with an Egyptian revolutionist group spreading false information about the government.

The Dilemma

  • Due to the negative impacts some users can have on the public or massive groups, Facebook has to censor some speech since they are a company.
  • There was also numerous situations where governments would use Facebook to silence or promote certain political speech or elections.
  • Due to Facebook's reach, they have massive implications on the outcomes of many political elections.
